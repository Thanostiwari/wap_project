VISION & MISSION




	OUR VISION – LEAVING GREEN FOOTPRINTS MILES AND MILES AWAY!

Our vision is to become a leader in web based tree planting services to build a green future for our future generations, 
provide them with a hospitable environment, and to set them free to breathe in a clean air by saving Mother Nature and planting 
more trees.

	OUR MISSION – TO TRANSFORM THE GESTURE OF “PLANTING TREES” A “THING TO CELEBRATE”!!!

Our Mission is to plant and grow as many trees to match at least 1 tree per person for the entire population in India. 
As soon we reached our previous mission, we will maintain the Yearly Child Birth & tree plantation ratio
		(No. of Tree planted in a year = No. of childbirth happened in a Year) 
to avoid burdening the future generations of ‘Trees’ who provide us with a new lease of life.
